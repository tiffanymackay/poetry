haiku

seaside air—
playground laughter
and coffee scent

wildflower countryside
waving back
at me

June’s solstice—
spring's farewell
and summer's greeting


sleepy seaside—
a seagull
announces its presence

Seattle spring—
a continuous
drizzle

midday sun—
amongst the wildflowers
sits a crow
