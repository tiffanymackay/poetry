haiku & micro poetry

seaside air—
playground laughter
and coffee scent

wildflower countryside
waving back
at me

June’s solstice—
spring's farewell
and summer's greeting


quiet cove—
a sailboat
sails past its mooring

Seattle spring—
a continuous
drizzle

midday sun—
amongst the wildflowers
sits a crow


snow-covered mountains
tucked into a bed of clouds—
having overslept

swaying side to side
ocean currents shift our course—
the whims of the sea

Spring clouds parting
opening to a vivid sky—
a world in color


early summer—
seaweed swaying back and forth
below the swirling waves

calm waters—
a sailboat
through the trees

sleepy seaside—
a seagull
announces its presence

tanka & longer poetry

i turned off
the well-worn
path into the
rugged terrain
learning to
forge ahead
and create
a path
i could
call
my
own

everyday I rise. everyday
i climb. everyday

i reach new heights. everyday
i soar. everyday

i shine brighter. everyday
i strive. everyday

i start anew. everyday
i try.

we all have
dark depths and
strong currents

we carry burdens and
wear scars

we triumph, we
fall, we
get up and
live
