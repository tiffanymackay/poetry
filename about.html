about tiffany liz mackay

tiffany is a designer by trade and a poet by calling. she lives in seattle, wa with her husband and two cats. she loves writing haikus, senryus, tankas, lunes, and other short form poetry.

having traveled to over twenty countries and territories in the last 10 years, she is passionate about sharing snippers from her journey across the globe.
